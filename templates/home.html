This is the home page for Yale Ride Coordinator. This website was created so that Yalies could easily find people to carpool with. So if you are looking to share an Uber to the airport with someone, look no further.

You can check out what other people's travel plans are on the Calendar page. If none of those appointments work for you, you can create your own. All appointments are anonymous until rides have been confirmed. When you request a ride, your email will be given to the creator of the appointment. If you accept someone's request to ride with you, your email will be given to them. When you join an appointment, all other members of that appointment will be able to see that you joined and your email.
